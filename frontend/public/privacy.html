Privacy Policy for Beatdrop

Last Updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Beatdrop ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our web application (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account or link your Google account to Beatdrop, we collect the following information:

  • Profile Information: Your name and email address provided by Google during authentication.
  • Calendar Data: If you authorize calendar synchronization, we request permission to manage your Google Calendar events. We only store the generated Event IDs to synchronize drops you track on Beatdrop.

2. How We Use Your Information

Beatdrop uses the limited information we collect exclusively to provide the core functionality of the Service:

  • To authenticate you and maintain your session securely.
  • To automatically create, update, or delete music release events on your personal Google Calendar based on the artists you choose to track.

3. Google User Data Policy

Beatdrop's use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

We do not use your Google Calendar data for advertising, nor do we sell, trade, or transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties.

4. Data Security & Deletion

Your Google API tokens are encrypted at rest in our database. You may revoke Beatdrop's access to your Google Calendar at any time from your Google Account settings, or by deleting your Beatdrop account, which will expunge your tokens from our systems.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the associated support channels.
